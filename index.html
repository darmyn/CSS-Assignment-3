Sour gummy bears are a delightful and tangy treat that has captured the taste buds of candy lovers around the world. These chewy, fruity, and sour candies are a popular choice for both kids and adults.

Interesting Fact #1

Sour gummy bears were invented in the 1980s by German candy maker Hans Riegel. The combination of sweet and sour flavors quickly gained popularity, and today, they are a staple in the candy world.

Interesting Fact #2

The sourness in gummy bears is often attributed to the use of citric acid, which not only provides the tangy flavor but also enhances the overall taste experience.

Nutritional Information
